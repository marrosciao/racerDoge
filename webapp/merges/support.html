Much Gameplay

  • Doge car keeps racing ahead, but use arrow keys to navigate bottom area of screen - left, right, up, or down.
  • Avoid all enemy cars - hitting them makes for a dead doge :(
  • Pick up Dogecoin to gain temporary armor (invincible wow!)
  • Pick up Pandacoin (PND) to shoot lasers temporarily (such science!)
    • Shooting an enemy car once wrecks it, but you still die if you collide with the wreck.
    • Shooting an enemy car twice destroys it completely (much rejoice!)
  • Pick up Bitcoin and Litecoin dropped by enemy cars for points!
  • Keep playing to beat your Wow Score (High Score)!

What is Dogecoin?

Dogecoin is an open source peer-to-peer cryptocurrency, favored by grammar-impaired Shiba Inus worldwide. Dogecoin is well known for having a large community of fun, positive, and generous users (shibes).

Check out the Dogecoin website to find out more!

What is Pandacoin (PND)?

Pandacoin (PND) is a crypto-currency that was created as a solution to the manipulative and dishonest forces in the crypto-currency community. The original goal of Pandacoin (PND) is to bring honesty, integrity and a strong sense of community into the ever-growing world of crypto-currencies. The Panda is a symbol of peace, positivity and compassion.

Check out the Pandacoin (PND) website or the FAQ Guide for Newcomers to find out more!
